====================
Cwf
====================

Cwf is a utility layer that sits on top of Django. It attempts to make it easier to create the urlpatterns, views and html for a menuing system.

To use cwf, it must be inside your pythonPath and you need ``cwf`` inside your ``settings.INSTALLED_APPS`` setting.

Read below to see what you can then achieve using this libary.
